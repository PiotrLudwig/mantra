Mantra

Everything will pass. So I keep the peace when the time is favorable. I keep calm when the time is unfavourable. This too will pass. I will always maintain the peace.

Everything that happens to me is a lesson that I accept with self-composure and from this I always go out winning.

Everything good and bad in my life is consequence of my previous actions. So I do not get angry because I'd have to be angry with myself first.
